About Me

My name is Jabrecia Washington. I am a 2nd year Game Design & Development student at Rochester Institute of Technology. Of course, I am interested in games, but I enjoy all kinds of media. Anything that anyone can experience and enjoy is something I thoroughly love. That includes movies, books, animations, music, websites, software, and, ultimately, games.

In this class, I hope to gain more knowledge in the field of web development since I've loved it for a long time. Outside of this class, I want to learn how to make something that anyone and everyone can enjoy, whether it be a game or a novel or a simple site like this one.

I'm not fully sure where I want to be when I graduate, hopefully somewhere fun and calm. I'd like to have a nice job where I can be creative with a bunch of amazing people. I'm open to a lot :)
