Warhammer Quest: Blackstone Fortress - Deadly Alliance


Description

Building off of the iconic Warhammer Quest: Blackstone Forest, Deadly Alliance introduces a brand new quest, a Trust mechanic to bring an interesting spin to your game, and a Zoat named The Archivist who comes wielding an atomic disassembler and an eradicator glove. This expansion will bring new life to an already lively tabletop game.
